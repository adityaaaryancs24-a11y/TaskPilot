Design a clean, modern desktop web app called "TaskPilot AI" — a personal AI task intelligence assistant for software engineers. This must NOT look like Jira, Linear, or a generic indigo/blue SaaS dashboard, and must NOT look like a generic "AI product" template (no plain glowing orb with floating dots and nothing else). Visual identity:

- Background: near-black (#0E1411), with a soft radial dark-green vignette (#16261D fading to #0E1411) for subtle depth, not flat black
- Surface cards: slightly lifted dark panel (#161D19) with hairline borders (#232B26), 10-12px rounded corners
- Signature accent color: sage/seafoam green (#8FCBA8, lighter variant #A8DABF, #B8E5CC) — used ONLY for the brand mark, AI-generated reasoning text, primary CTAs, live-status indicators, and active nav states. Do not use it decoratively elsewhere.
- Priority/status tags use their own semantic colors at low opacity (12-15% bg, full-saturation text, thin matching border): red-coral (#F09595) for P0/critical, amber (#EF9F27) for P1/high, sage for "ready/low" — these stay visually distinct from the brand accent
- Logo mark: small rounded-square icon (48px), dark green gradient fill (#2E5C44 to #16271E), thin border (#3D6B52), sparkle symbol in light sage (#B8E5CC) centered inside, with a soft pulsing glow halo behind it
- Typography: Inter, sentence case everywhere, near-white primary text (#F4F8F5, never pure white), muted gray-green secondary text (#6B7A72 to #94A39B)
- A recurring "live system" motif: small status lines in monospace font, low-opacity pulsing dots indicating active scanning/processing — this is what makes the product feel alive and working, not just decorated
- Every AI-generated insight, rationale, or "why this rank" explanation is marked with a small sage sparkle icon and sage-tinted text, distinguishing AI reasoning from raw source data and from status tags
- Overall feel: calm but alive — a premium personal AI tool that visibly feels like it's actively working on your behalf, not a static enterprise dashboard
